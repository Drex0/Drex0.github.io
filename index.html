About this guy

I have worked in the Audio/Video industry for 13 years integrating systems in Government, Corporate, Education, Hospitality, and Retail. I've been involved in everything from designing systems, installation, rack fabrication, audio and control programming. I am proficient in many different software and hardware platforms, below are a few I use regularly. I am employed at Harman International and specialize in BSS Audio supporting Large Venue arenas and facilities.

BSS and Crestron v4.2

This is a Simpl+ module I created to work with the existing 4.2 modules BSS offers. This is used to control the BLU103 and I also provide an example file. I utilize the new DI String protocol for setting and getting the phone number, caller ID, and speed dials.
